O1 — Law of the Obvious

The strongest signals eventually become impossible to ignore.

Or:

Reality becomes obvious after sufficient saturation.

This law is about:

seeing the current regime clearly
recognizing collective behavior
noticing what has become excessive
identifying where attention has concentrated

Examples:

endless podcasts
AI everywhere
hustle culture
over-optimization
hyper-digitization
maximalist content
institutional distrust

The point is:
before inversion comes saturation.

You must first see:

what the world has already become.

That’s O1.


O2 — Law of Opposites

Saturation creates demand for the inverse.

Or:

Every dominant cycle creates its emotional opposite.

This is where asymmetry emerges.

Examples:

maximalism → minimalism
crypto decentralization → AI centralization
glamour → grunge
abundance → curation
noise → silence
endless talking → “say less”

This law explains:

where the next wave forms.

O3 — Law of Outliers

Every major cycle concentrates rewards into a few dominant winners.

Or:

Power-law systems reward the outlier disproportionately.

This is:

Bitcoin in crypto
Nvidia in AI
Facebook in social
Amazon in ecommerce
Apple in smartphones

Most people identify:

the trend

Few identify:

the dominant compounding vehicle.
