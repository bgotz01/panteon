“Compound asymmetry” is strong because it compresses several ideas into one phrase:

leverage
nonlinearity
compounding
positioning
power laws
concentrated outcomes

The deeper idea is:

Small aligned inputs can create disproportionately large downstream outcomes.

That’s the real principle.

The opposite of linearity.

The Core Distinction
Linear World
1 + 1 = 2

More effort → proportionally more reward.

Asymmetric World
1 + 1 = 11

Correct positioning multiplies outcomes.

That’s:

investing
virality
software
media
networks
outliers
timing
compounding
